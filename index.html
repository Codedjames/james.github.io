About Me

James is a Google Certified Data Analyst, with a strong foundation in data analytics and business intelligence. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science (Information Systems option) from the Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA)

With a passion for transforming data into actionable insights, James has honed his skills in data analytics, data visualization, product analytics, and market research. He specializes in uncovering trends and patterns that drive strategic business decisions, leveraging advanced analytical tools and techniques to deliver clear, impactful visualizations and reports. James is dedicated to helping organizations optimize their products and understand market dynamics through data-driven approaches, ensuring measurable business growth and innovation.

With over three years of expertise in the data arena, he has excelled not only as a Data Analytics instructor, but has also taken on roles in analytics and big data projects in the fintech space. His recent endeavours include building analytics solutions and enhancing his portfolio with hands-on experience in product analytics at BrandDrive.

James is proficient in a wide range of data analytics and business intelligence tools. He is highly skilled in Power BI for interactive dashboards and data visualization, and adept at using Microsoft Excel for advanced data manipulation and analysis. His expertise extends to SQL for querying and managing databases, as well as Python for data analysis, automation, and scripting. James is also experienced with Looker Studio and Google BigQuery for scalable data processing and reporting, and utilizes Mixpanel for product analytics and user behavior tracking. This diverse toolkit enables him to deliver comprehensive, data-driven solutions tailored to business needs.
